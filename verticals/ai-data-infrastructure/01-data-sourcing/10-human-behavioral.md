# 10. Human Behavioral / Telemetry

## What Goes In

Every click, swipe, scroll, pause, tap, voice command, and biometric signal generated by humans interacting with digital systems.

## What Comes Out

Behavioral profiles, engagement metrics, conversion funnels, and training data for recommendation engines, conversational AI, and user experience optimization.

## Open vs. Proprietary

Almost entirely **proprietary and locked inside platforms**. The few open behavioral datasets (MovieLens for recommendations, Last.fm for music) are research releases, not live feeds. GDPR and app tracking transparency (iOS) are making this data harder to collect.

## The Stalwart

**MovieLens**. A small research project at the University of Minnesota that has collected 20+ million movie ratings from 160,000+ users since 1997. Completely free, completely anonymized, and the standard benchmark for recommendation system research for over two decades. No ads, no tracking, no monetization.
