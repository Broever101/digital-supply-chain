# 4. Satellite & Geospatial

## What Goes In

Photons hitting sensors on satellites, drones, and aircraft. Raw pixels orbiting the Earth at 500+ kilometers altitude.

## What Comes Out

Georeferenced imagery and analytics: crop health maps, deforestation alerts, urban growth tracking, disaster damage assessment, and training data for geospatial AI.

## Open vs. Proprietary

**Mixed**. Government programs like Sentinel and Landsat provide free, open data. Commercial providers (Planet, Maxar) sell high-resolution imagery at premium prices. The analytics layer on top is almost entirely proprietary.

## The Stalwart

**Sentinel-2 (ESA)**. A constellation of satellites operated by the European Space Agency that provides free, high-resolution multispectral imagery of the entire planet every 5 days. Climate researchers, farmers, and startups build billion-dollar insights on top of this completely free infrastructure.
