# 11. Multimedia / Content Libraries

## What Goes In

Professionally produced images, videos, audio clips, and text content created by photographers, filmmakers, musicians, journalists, and designers.

## What Comes Out

Licensed datasets used to train image generation models (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion), video synthesis, music generation, and multimodal understanding.

## Open vs. Proprietary

**Mixed and legally fraught**. LAION-5B (used to train Stable Diffusion) was built by scraping the web without explicit consent. Getty Images is suing Stability AI for unauthorized use of its content. Adobe and Shutterstock now sell "AI training licenses" as a new revenue stream. The legal landscape is shifting daily.

## The Stalwart

**LAION**. A German non-profit with no office, no full-time employees, and no funding that curated one of the largest image-text datasets in the world (LAION-5B: 5.85 billion image-text pairs). Used to train Stable Diffusion and countless other models. Built entirely by volunteers scraping public web data. The ultimate underdog that accidentally became the foundation of the generative AI revolution.
