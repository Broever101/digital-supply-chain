# 9. Financial / Market Data

## What Goes In

Every trade, quote, order book update, news headline, earnings report, and macroeconomic indicator generated by global financial markets.

## What Comes Out

Time-series datasets used to train trading algorithms, risk models, fraud detection systems, and economic forecasting models.

## Open vs. Proprietary

Almost entirely **proprietary and expensive**. Real-time exchange data costs thousands of dollars per month. Historical tick data is a premium product. The only "open" financial data is delayed quotes and government macroeconomic releases.

## The Stalwart

**IEX Cloud**. Born from the Investors Exchange (IEX), a stock exchange designed to protect investors from high-frequency trading predators. They launched a data API with the radical premise that stock market data should be affordable for startups and individual developers, not just banks with Bloomberg terminals. They undercut incumbents by 10x on price.
