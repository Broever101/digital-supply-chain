# 2. Proprietary / Commercial Data Marketplaces

## What Goes In

Curated, cleaned, and often licensed datasets from companies that specialize in collecting or generating specific types of data. This is not raw web scrapes — it is data sold as a product.

## What Comes Out

Queryable datasets that appear directly inside a company's cloud warehouse, ready for analysis or model training without messy ingestion pipelines.

## Open vs. Proprietary

Almost entirely **proprietary**. These are businesses built on controlling access to data. The exception: some government datasets hosted on these platforms are free to access.

## The Stalwart

**Hugging Face Datasets**. Not a marketplace in the traditional sense, but a community-driven repository where researchers and companies share datasets openly. It has become the default place to publish and discover datasets, and it is run by a relatively small team with a mission to democratize AI.
